<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hither hither, love
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;'Tis a shady mead
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hither, hither, love!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Let us feed and feed!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hither, hither, sweet
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;'Tis a cowslip bed
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hither, hither, sweet!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;'Tis with dew bespread!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hither, hither, dear
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By the breath of life,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hither, hither, dear!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Be the summer's wife!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Though one moment's pleasure
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In one moment flies
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Though the passion's treasure
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In one moment dies;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yet it has not passed,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Think how near, how near!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And while it doth last,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Think how dear, how dear!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hither, hither, hither
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Love its boon has sent
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If I die and wither
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I shall die content!
</pre>
