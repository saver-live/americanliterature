<p>Chang-an -- one slip of moon;<br> in ten thousand houses, the sound of fulling mallets.<br> Autumn winds keep on blowing,<br> all things make me think of Jade Pass!<br> When will they put down the barbarians<br> and my good man come home from his far campaign? <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
