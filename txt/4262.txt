<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Bards of Passion and of Mirth,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ye have left your souls on earth!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Have ye souls in heaven too,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Double-lived in regions new?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yes, and those of heaven commune
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With the spheres of sun and moon;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With the noise of fountains wondrous,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the parle of voices thundrous;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With the whisper of heavens trees
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And one another, in soft ease
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Seated on Elysian lawns
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Browsd by none but Dians fawns;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Underneath large blue-bells tented,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where the daisies are rose-scented,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the rose herself has got
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Perfume which on earth is not;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where the nightingale doth sing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Not a senseless, tranced thing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But divine melodious truth;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Philosophic numbers smooth;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tales and golden histories
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of heaven and its mysteries.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thus ye live on high, and then
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On the earth ye live again;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the souls ye left behind you
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Teach us, here, the way to find you,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where your other souls are joying,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Never slumberd, never cloying.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Here, your earth-born souls still speak
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To mortals, of their little week;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of their sorrows and delights;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of their passions and their spites;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of their glory and their shame;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What doth strengthen and what maim.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thus ye teach us, every day,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Wisdom, though fled far away.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Bards of Passion and of Mirth,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ye have left your souls on earth!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ye have souls in heaven too,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Double-lived in regions new!
</pre>
