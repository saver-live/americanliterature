<p><cite class="al-title">The Story of Daniel Webster</cite> was published in Baldwin's book for young Americans, <cite class="al-title">Four Great Americans: Washington, Franklin, Webster, Lincoln</cite> (1897). Webster was a Congressman and Senator who became known as the ablest lawyer and advocate in America after winning the Dartmouth College case. Above all, he was remembered for his great speeches and patriotism.
