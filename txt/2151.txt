<p>I FIX mine eye on thine, and there<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Pity my picture burning in thine eye;<br> My picture drown'd in a transparent tear,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>When I look lower I espy;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>Hadst thou the wicked skill<br> By pictures made and marr'd, to kill,<br> How many ways mightst thou perform thy will?<br> <br> But now I've drunk thy sweet salt tears,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And though thou pour more, I'll depart;<br> My picture vanished, vanish all fears<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>That I can be endamaged by that art;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>Though thou retain of me<br> One picture more, yet that will be,<br> Being in thine own heart, from all malice free.<br></p>
