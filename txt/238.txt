<p>'Is this century worse than those before?'<br> Is this century really worse than those before?<br> Perhaps, in that dazed by fear and grief,<br> It touched a blackest sore<br> It could not heal.<br> <br> In the west the earthly sun shines yet,<br> And city roofs gleam in its light,<br> But here the white one marks doors with crosses,<br> Summons the crows, and the crows are in flight.<br></p>
