<pre>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Each heart has its haunted chamber,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where the silent moonlight falls!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On the floor are mysterious footsteps,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There are whispers along the walls!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And mine at times is haunted
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By phantoms of the Past
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As motionless as shadows
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By the silent moonlight cast.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A form sits by the window,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That is not seen by day,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For as soon as the dawn approaches
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It vanishes away.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It sits there in the moonlight
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Itself as pale and still,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And points with its airy finger
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Across the window-sill.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Without before the window,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There stands a gloomy pine,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Whose boughs wave upward and downward
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As wave these thoughts of mine.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And underneath its branches
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Is the grave of a little child,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Who died upon life's threshold,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And never wept nor smiled.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What are ye, O pallid phantoms!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That haunt my troubled brain?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That vanish when day approaches,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And at night return again?

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What are ye, O pallid phantoms!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But the statues without breath,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That stand on the bridge overarching
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The silent river of death?
</pre>
