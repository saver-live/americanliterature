<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When descends on the Atlantic
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The gigantic
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Storm-wind of the equinox,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Landward in his wrath he scourges
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The toiling surges,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Laden with seaweed from the rocks:

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From Bermuda's reefs; from edges
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Of sunken ledges,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In some far-off, bright Azore;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From Bahama, and the dashing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Silver-flashing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Surges of San Salvador;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the tumbling surf, that buries
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The Orkneyan skerries,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Answering the hoarse Hebrides;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And from wrecks of ships, and drifting
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Spars, uplifting
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On the desolate, rainy seas;--

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ever drifting, drifting, drifting
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; On the shifting
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Currents of the restless main;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Till in sheltered coves, and reaches
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Of sandy beaches,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All have found repose again.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So when storms of wild emotion
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Strike the ocean
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the poet's soul, erelong
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From each cave and rocky fastness,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; In its vastness,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Floats some fragment of a song:

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Front the far-off isles enchanted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Heaven has planted
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With the golden fruit of Truth;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the flashing surf, whose vision
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Gleams Elysian
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the tropic clime of Youth;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the strong Will, and the Endeavor
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; That forever
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Wrestle with the tides of Fate
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the wreck of Hopes far-scattered,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Tempest-shattered,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Floating waste and desolate;--

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ever drifting, drifting, drifting
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; On the shifting
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Currents of the restless heart;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Till at length in books recorded,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; They, like hoarded
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Household words, no more depart.
</pre>
