<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of Prometheus, how undaunted
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On Olympus' shining bastions
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;His audacious foot he planted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Myths are told and songs are chanted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Full of promptings and suggestions.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Beautiful is the tradition
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of that flight through heavenly portals,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The old classic superstition
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the theft and the transmission
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the fire of the Immortals!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;First the deed of noble daring,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Born of heavenward aspiration,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then the fire with mortals sharing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then the vulture,--the despairing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Cry of pain on crags Caucasian.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All is but a symbol painted
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the Poet, Prophet, Seer;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Only those are crowned and sainted
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Who with grief have been acquainted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Making nations nobler, freer.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In their feverish exultations,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In their triumph and their yearning,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In their passionate pulsations,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In their words among the nations,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Promethean fire is burning.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Shall it, then, be unavailing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All this toil for human culture?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through the cloud-rack, dark and trailing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Must they see above them sailing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O'er life's barren crags the vulture?

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Such a fate as this was Dante's,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By defeat and exile maddened;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thus were Milton and Cervantes,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nature's priests and Corybantes,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By affliction touched and saddened.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But the glories so transcendent
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That around their memories cluster,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And, on all their steps attendant,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Make their darkened lives resplendent
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With such gleams of inward lustre!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All the melodies mysterious,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through the dreary darkness chanted;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thoughts in attitudes imperious,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Voices soft, and deep, and serious,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Words that whispered, songs that haunted!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All the soul in rapt suspension,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All the quivering, palpitating
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Chords of life in utmost tension,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With the fervor of invention,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With the rapture of creating!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ah, Prometheus! heaven-scaling!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In such hours of exultation
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Even the faintest heart, unquailing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Might behold the vulture sailing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Round the cloudy crags Caucasian!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Though to all there is not given
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Strength for such sublime endeavor,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thus to scale the walls of heaven,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And to leaven with fiery leaven
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All the hearts of men for ever;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yet all bards, whose hearts unblighted
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Honor and believe the presage,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hold aloft their torches lighted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Gleaming through the realms benighted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As they onward bear the message!
</pre>
