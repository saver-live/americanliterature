<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Now hollow fires burn out to black,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And lights are guttering low:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Square your shoulders, lift your pack,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And leave your friends and go.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Oh never fear, man, nought's to dread,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Look not left nor right:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In all the endless road you tread
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There's nothing but the night.
</pre>
