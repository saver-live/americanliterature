<p><cite class="al-title">The Phantom of the Opera</cite> (1910) was inspired by historical events that occurred in the Paris Opera during the 18th century. <cite class="al-title">Le Fantôme de l'Opéra</cite> was first published as a serial in the magazine, <cite class="al-periodical">Le Gaulois</cite> from September 1909, to January 1910. It has been adapted by <cite class="al-person">Andrew Lloyd Webber</cite> into one of the longest-running and popular operas, performed throughout the world.
