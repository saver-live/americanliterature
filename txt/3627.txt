<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When I came last to Ludlow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Amidst the moonlight pale,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Two friends kept step beside me,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Two honest lads and hale.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Now Dick lies long in the churchyard,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And Ned lies long in jail,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And I come home to Ludlow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Amidst the moonlight pale.
