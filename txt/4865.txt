<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Such as in Ships and brittle Barks
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Into the Seas descend
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Shall learn how wholly on those Arks
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Our Victuals do depend.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For, when a Man would bite or sup,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Or buy him Goods or Gear,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He needs must call the Oceans up,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And move an Hemisphere.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Consider, now, that Indian Weed
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Which groweth oer the Main,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With Teas and Cottons for our Need,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And Sugar of the Cane,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Their Comings We no more regard
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Than daily Corn or Oil:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yet, when Men waft Them Englandward,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How infinite the Toil!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nation and People harvesteth
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The tropique Lands among,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And Engines of tumultuous Breath
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Do draw the Yield along,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yea, even as by Hecatombs
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Which, presently struck down
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Into our Navies labouring Wombs
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Make Pennyworths in Town.
</pre>
