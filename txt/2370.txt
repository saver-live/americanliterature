<p><em><b>True Stories of Girl Heroines</b></em> by Evelyn Everett-Green is a collection of historical sketches about real-life female heroines and how their bold and courageous actions decided the outcomes of their respective situations. </p><p> Profiled in this book are: Inez Arroya, Catharine The Rose, Elsje Van Houwening, Grizel Cochrane, Eva Von Gross, Emma Fitz-Osborn, Elizabeth Stuart, Charlotte Honeyman, Mary Bridges, Theresa Duroc, Jane Lane, Helen Kottenner, Maid Lillyard, Margaret Wilson, Agostina Of Zaragoza, Agnes Beaumont, Hannah Hewling, Mona Drummond, Jessy Varcoe, and
