<p>Wilt thou forgive that sin where I begun,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Which is my sin, though it were done before?<br> Wilt thou forgive that sin through which I run,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And do run still, though still I do deplore?<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>When thou hast done, thou hast not done,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:3em;">&nbsp;</span>For I have more.<br> <br> Wilt thou forgive that sin by which I have won<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Others to sin? and made my sin their door?<br> Wilt thou forgive that sin which I did shun<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>A year or two, but wallowed in a score?<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>When thou hast done, thou hast not done,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:3em;">&nbsp;</span>For I have more.<br> <br> I have a sin of fear, that when I have spun<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>My last thread, I shall perish on the shore;<br> Swear by thy self, that at my death thy Son<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Shall shine as he shines now and heretofore;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>And, having done that, thou hast done,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:3em;">&nbsp;</span>I fear no more.<br> <br></p>
