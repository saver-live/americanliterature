<p><cite class="al-title">Whose Body?"</cite> (1923) is English crime writer, Dorothy Sayers' first novel to enter the US public domain (Jan. 2019). She introduces her most famous character, Lord Peter Wimsey, in his first case. Subtitle: <i>"The Singular Adventure of the Man with the Golden Pince-Nez"</i> [Book cover illustration by Eugene Ivanov.]</p><p><i>To M.
