<pre>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I heard the bells on Christmas Day
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Their old, familiar carols play,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; And wild and sweet
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The words repeat
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of peace on earth, good-will to men!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And thought how, as the day had come,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The belfries of all Christendom
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Had rolled along
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The unbroken song
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of peace on earth, good-will to men!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Till, ringing, singing on its way,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The world revolved from night to day,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A voice, a chime,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; A chant sublime
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of peace on earth, good-will to men!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then from each black, accursed mouth
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The cannon thundered in the South,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; And with the sound
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The carols drowned
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of peace on earth, good-will to men!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It was as if an earthquake rent
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The hearth-stones of a continent,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; And made forlorn
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The households born
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of peace on earth, good-will to men!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And in despair I bowed my head;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"There is no peace on earth," I said:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; "For hate is strong,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; And mocks the song
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of peace on earth, good-will to men!"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then pealed the bells more loud and deep:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"God is not dead; nor doth he sleep!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The Wrong shall fail,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The Right prevail,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With peace on earth, good-will to men!"
</pre>
