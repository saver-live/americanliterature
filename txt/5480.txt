<p>Hugh Lofting's sequel to <a href="https://americanliterature.com/author/hugh-lofting/book/the-story-of-doctor-dolittle/summary"><span class="al-title">The Story of Doctor Dolittle</span></a>, which first introduced us to the very special man who could talk to the animals and come to their aid. <span class="al-title">The Voyages of Doctor Dolittle</span> takes us to Polynesia and beyond, far from his home of Puddleby-on-the-Marsh. The book, written and illustrated by Lofting, was published in 1922. Its subtitle, "The Popsipetel Picture-History of King John Thinkalot," invites us to embark on the doctor's fanciful adventures. It won the Newbery Medal in 1923.</p><p>This book is featured in our collection of <a href="/books-for-young-readers">Books for Young Readers</a>.</p><p><a href="prologue">Prologue</a></p><p><a href="part-one-the-first-chapter-the-first-chapter-the-cobblers-son">Part One -
