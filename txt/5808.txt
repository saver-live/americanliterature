<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Four limpid lakes,--four Naiades
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Or sylvan deities are these,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In flowing robes of azure dressed;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Four lovely handmaids, that uphold
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Their shining mirrors, rimmed with gold,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To the fair city in the West.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By day the coursers of the sun
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Drink of these waters as they run
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Their swift diurnal round on high;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By night the constellations glow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Far down the hollow deeps below,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And glimmer in another sky.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fair lakes, serene and full of light,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fair town, arrayed in robes of white,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How visionary ye appear!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All like a floating landscape seems
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In cloud-land or the land of dreams,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Bathed in a golden atmosphere!
</pre>
