<p><cite class="al-title">Rob Roy</cite> was Scott's popular adventure novel, considered the magnus opus of his Waverley novels, published in 1817. It is considered a quintessential work in the genre of <a href="/romanticism-study-guide">Romanticism</a>, inspiring countless tales of swashbuckling and chivalry ever since.
