<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Awake! arise! the hour is late!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Angels are knocking at thy door!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They are in haste and cannot wait,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And once departed come no more.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Awake! arise! the athlete's arm
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Loses its strength by too much rest;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The fallow land, the untilled farm
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Produces only weeds at best.
</pre>
