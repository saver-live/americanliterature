<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sweet chimes! that in the loneliness of night
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Salute the passing hour, and in the dark
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And silent chambers of the household mark
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The movements of the myriad orbs of light!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through my closed eyelids, by the inner sight,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I see the constellations in the arc
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of their great circles moving on, and hark!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I almost hear them singing in their flight.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Better than sleep it is to lie awake
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O'er-canopied by the vast starry dome
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the immeasurable sky; to feel
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The slumbering world sink under us, and make
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hardly an eddy,--a mere rush of foam
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On the great sea beneath a sinking keel.
</pre>
