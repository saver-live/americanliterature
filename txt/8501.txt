<p>Twain's popular novel chronicles the adventures of two young boys, a Prince and a Pauper, who exchange roles and stations in life. Each boy has strong misconception's of what the other boys life is like and series of educational and entertaining adventures play out as the boys grow more comfortable in both their real and assumed roles in life. Mark Twain's Prince and the Pauper is a popular story and a classic from American Literature.</p><p>This book is featured in our collection of <a href="/books-for-young-readers">Books for Young Readers</a> and <a href="/twenty-five-great-american-novels">25 Great American Novels</a></p><p><a href="chapter-i-the-birth-of-the-prince-and-the-pauper">Chapter I.
