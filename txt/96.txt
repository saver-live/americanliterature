<p>A very young Fox, who had never before seen a Lion, happened to meet one in the forest. A single look was enough to send the Fox off at top speed for the nearest hiding place.</p><p>The second time the Fox saw the Lion he stopped behind a tree to look at him a moment before slinking away. But the third time, the Fox went boldly up to the Lion and, without turning a hair, said, "Hello, there, old top."</p><p class="center"><i>Familiarity breeds contempt.</i></p><p class="center"><i>Acquaintance with evil blinds us to its dangers.</i></p><p> </p><p><br> </p><p> </p>
