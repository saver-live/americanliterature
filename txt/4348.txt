<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I cry your mercy, pity, love! aye, love!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Merciful love that tantalizes not,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;One-thoughted, never-wandering, guileless love,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Unmasked, and being seen, without a blot!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O! let me have thee whole, all, all, be mine!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That shape, that fairness, that sweet minor zest
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of love, your kiss, those hands, those eyes divine,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That warm, white, lucent, million-pleasured breast, 
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yourself, your soul, in pity give me all,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Withhold no atom's atom or I die,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Or living on, perhaps, your wretched thrall,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Forget, in the mist of idle misery,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Life's purposes, the palate of my mind
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Losing its gust, and my ambition blind!
</pre>
