<p><cite class="al-title">The Wonder</cite> is the shorted title from the original, <cite class="al-title">The Hampdenshire Wonder</cite>, first published in 1911. It is one of the first science fiction novels describing a wunderkind. Victor Stott, the son of a famous cricket player, has a slight deformity accounting for his extraordinarily powerful brain. The character may be Beresford's reference to his mentor, <a href="/author/hg-wells">H.G.
