<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On the idle hill of summer,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sleepy with the flow of streams,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Far I hear the steady drummer
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Drumming like a noise in dreams.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Far and near and low and louder
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On the roads of earth go by,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Dear to friends and food for powder,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Soldiers marching, all to die.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;East and west on fields forgotten
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Bleach the bones of comrades slain,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lovely lads and dead and rotten;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;None that go return again.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Far the calling bugles hollo,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;High the screaming fife replies,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Gay the files of scarlet follow:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Woman bore me, I will rise.
</pre>
