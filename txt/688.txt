<p>You ask how I spend my time--<br> I nestle against a treetrunk<br> and listen to autumn winds<br> in the pines all night and day.<br> <br> Shantung wine can't get me drunk.<br> The local poets bore me.<br> My thoughts remain with you,<br> like the Wen River, endlessly flowing.<br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
