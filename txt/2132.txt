<p>WHERE, like a pillow on a bed,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>A pregnant bank swell'd up, to rest<br> The violet's reclining head,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Our eyes upon one double string.<br> <br> So to engraft our hands, as yet<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Was all the means to make us one;<br> And pictures in our eyes to get<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Was all our propagation.<br> <br> As, 'twixt two equal armies, Fate<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Suspends uncertain victory,<br> Our souls—which to advance their state,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Were gone out—hung 'twixt her and me.<br> <br> And whilst our souls negotiate there,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>We like sepulchral statues lay;<br> All day, the same our postures were,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And we said nothing, all the day.<br> <br> If any, so by love refined,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>That he soul's language understood,<br> And by good love were grown all mind,<br> <span style="display:inline-block;
