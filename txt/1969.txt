<p><cite class="al-title">A Child's History of England</cite> was published as a serial in Dicken's magazine, <cite class="al-periodical">Household Words</cite> between 1851-1853. He summarizes in separate chapters the reigns of England's monarchy, from Henry III to Richard III.
