<p> Originally publised under the title <em>A Lady</em> in 1811, <em><b>Sense and Sensibility</b></em> was Jane Austen's first published work. Remarkably, she wrote the first draft in 1795 when she was only 19 years old. The original version was drafted as a novel-in-letters and entitled <em>Elinor and Marianne</em>. </p><p> A romantic fiction, <em><b>Sense and Sensibility</b></em> is set in southwest England in the 1790s and follows Elinor and Marianne Dashwood as they experience love, romance and heartbreak. </p><p><a href="chapter-1">Chapter 1</a></p><p><a href="chapter-2">Chapter 2</a></p><p><a
