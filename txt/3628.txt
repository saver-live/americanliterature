<pre>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Far I hear the bugle blow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To call me where I would not go,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the guns begin the song,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;'Soldier, fly or stay for long.'"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Comrade, if to turn and fly
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Made a soldier never die,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fly I would, for who would not?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;'Tis sure no pleasure to be shot."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"But since the man that runs away
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lives to die another day,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And cowards' funerals, when they come
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Are not wept so well at home."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Therefore, though the best is bad,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Stand and do the best my lad;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Stand and fight and see your slain,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And take the bullet in your brain."
