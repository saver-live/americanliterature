<p><cite class="al-title">Beyond the Horizon</cite> is a play in three acts which explores the dynamics of love when two men love the same woman. It earned O'Neill the Pulitzer Prize for Drama in 1920 and is featured in our collection of <a href="/pulitzer-prizes#pulitzer-drama">Pulitzer Prizes</a>.
