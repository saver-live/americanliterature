<pre>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The day is ending,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The night is descending;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The marsh is frozen,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The river dead.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through clouds like ashes
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The red sun flashes
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On village windows
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That glimmer red.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The snow recommences;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The buried fences
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Mark no longer
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The road o'er the plain;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;While through the meadows,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Like fearful shadows,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Slowly passes
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A funeral train.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The bell is pealing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And every feeling
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Within me responds
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To the dismal knell;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Shadows are trailing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;My heart is bewailing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And tolling within
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Like a funeral bell.
</pre>
