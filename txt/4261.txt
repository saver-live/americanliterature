<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blue! 'Tis the life of heaven, the domain
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of Cynthia, the wide palace of the sun, 
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The tent of Hesperus, and all his train, 
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The bosomer of clouds, gold, gray, and dun.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blue! 'Tis the life of waters: Ocean
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And all its vassal streams, pools numberless,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;May rage, and foam, and fret, but never can
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Subside, if not to dark-blue nativeness.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blue! Gentle cousin of the forest-green,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Married to green in all the sweetest flowers, 
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Forget-me-not, the Blue bell, and, that Queen
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of secrecy, the Violet: what strange powers
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hast thou, as a mere shadow! But how great,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When in an Eye thou art alive with fate!
</pre>
