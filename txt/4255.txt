<pre>1.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Unfelt unheard, unseen,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I've left my little queen,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Her languid arms in silver slumber lying:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ah! through their nestling touch,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Who, who could tell how much
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There is for madness, cruel, or complying?

2.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Those faery lids how sleek!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Those lips how moist! they speak,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In ripest quiet, shadows of sweet sounds:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Into my fancy's ear
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Melting a burden dear,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How "Love doth know no fullness, nor no bounds."

3.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;True, tender monitors!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I bend unto your laws:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This sweetest day for dalliance was born!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So, without more ado,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I'll feel my heaven anew,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For all the blushing of the hasty morn.
</pre>
