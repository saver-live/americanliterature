<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O precious evenings! all too swiftly sped!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Leaving us heirs to amplest heritages
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of all the best thoughts of the greatest sages,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And giving tongues unto the silent dead!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How our hearts glowed and trembled as she read,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Interpreting by tones the wondrous pages
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the great poet who foreruns the ages,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Anticipating all that shall be said!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O happy Reader! having for thy text
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The magic book, whose Sibylline leaves have caught
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The rarest essence of all human thought!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O happy Poet! by no critic vext!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How must thy listening spirit now rejoice
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To be interpreted by such a voice!
</pre>
