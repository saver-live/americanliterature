<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Becalmed upon the sea of Thought,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Still unattained the land it sought,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;My mind, with loosely-hanging sails,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lies waiting the auspicious gales.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On either side, behind, before,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The ocean stretches like a floor,--
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A level floor of amethyst,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crowned by a golden dome of mist.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blow, breath of inspiration, blow!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Shake and uplift this golden glow!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And fill the canvas of the mind
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With wafts of thy celestial wind.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blow, breath of song! until I feel
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The straining sail, the lifting keel,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The life of the awakening sea,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Its motion and its mystery!
</pre>
