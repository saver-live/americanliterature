<p>If you sit down at set of sun<br> And count the acts that you have done,<br> And, counting, find<br> One self-denying deed, one word<br> That eased the heart of him who heard,<br> One glance most kind<br> That fell like sunshine where it went--<br> Then you may count that day well spent.<br> <br> But if, through all the livelong day,<br> You've cheered no heart, by yea or nay--<br> If, through it all<br> You've nothing done that you can trace<br> That brought the sunshine to one face--<br> No act most small<br> That helped some soul and nothing cost--<br> Then count that day as worse than lost.</p><p>If you sit down at set of sun<br> And count the acts that you have done,<br> And, counting, find<br> One self-denying deed, one word<br> That eased the heart of him who heard,<br> One glance most kind<br> That fell like sunshine where it went--<br> Then you may count that day well spent.<br> <br> But if, through all the livelong day,<br> You've cheered no heart, by yea or nay--<br> If, through it all<br> You've nothing done that you can trace<br> That brought the sunshine to one face--<br> No act most small<br> That helped some soul and nothing cost--<br> Then count that day as worse than lost.</p>
