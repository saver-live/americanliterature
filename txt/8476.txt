<p><cite class="al-title">A Sportsman's Sketches</cite> (1852), alternatively titled <cite class="al-title">Sketches from a Hunter's Album</cite>, or <cite class="al-title">Notes of a Hunter</cite>. is a collection of short stories establishing Turgenev as a master of Russian Realism, prior to publication of his novel considered his masterwork, <a href="/author/ivan-s-turgenev/book/fathers-and-sons/summary">Fathers and Sons</a>, published in 1862.
