<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thou Royal River, born of sun and shower
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In chambers purple with the Alpine glow,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Wrapped in the spotless ermine of the snow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And rocked by tempests!--at the appointed hour
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Forth, like a steel-clad horseman from a tower,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With clang and clink of harness dost thou go
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To meet thy vassal torrents, that below
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Rush to receive thee and obey thy power.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And now thou movest in triumphal march,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A king among the rivers!&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On thy way
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A hundred towns await and welcome thee;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Bridges uplift for thee the stately arch,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Vineyards encircle thee with garlands gay,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And fleets attend thy progress to the sea!
</pre>
