<p>"Why in the world do you walk sideways like that?" said a Mother Crab to her son. "You should always walk straight forward with your toes turned out."</p><p>"Show me how to walk, mother dear," answered the little Crab obediently, "I want to learn."</p><p>So the old Crab tried and <i>tried</i> to walk straight forward. But she could walk sideways only, like her son. And when she wanted to turn her toes out she tripped and fell on her nose.</p><p class="center"><i>Do not tell others how to act unless you can set a good example.</i></p><p></p><p><br></p><p></p>
