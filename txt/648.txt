<p>White King City I left at dawn<br> in the morning-glow of the clouds;<br> The thousand miles to Chiang-ling<br> we sailed in a single day.<br> On either shore the gibbons' chatter<br> sounded without pause<br> While my light boat skimmed past<br> ten thousand sombre crags. <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
