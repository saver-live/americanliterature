<p> Serialised from September 1914 to May 1915 in the <em>Strand Magazine</em>, <em><b>The Valley of Fear</b></em>, is the fourth and final Sherlock Holmes novel by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. This installment is base on the real life exploits of the Molly Maguires and Pinkerton agent James McParland.
