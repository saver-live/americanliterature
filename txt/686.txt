<p>Visiting the nun Rise-In-Air,<br> You must be near her place in those blue hills.<br> The river's force helps pound the mica,<br> The wind washes rose bay tree flowers.<br> If you find you can't leave that refuge,<br> Invite me there to see the sunset's fire. <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
