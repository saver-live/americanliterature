<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O sweet illusions of Song,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That tempt me everywhere,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the lonely fields, and the throng
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the crowded thoroughfare!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I approach, and ye vanish away,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I grasp you, and ye are gone;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But ever by nigh an day,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The melody soundeth on.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As the weary traveller sees
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In desert or prairie vast,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blue lakes, overhung with trees,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That a pleasant shadow cast;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fair towns with turrets high,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And shining roofs of gold,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That vanish as he draws nigh,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Like mists together rolled,--

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So I wander and wander along,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And forever before me gleams
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The shining city of song,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the beautiful land of dreams.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But when I would enter the gate
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of that golden atmosphere,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It is gone, and I wander and wait
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For the vision to reappear.
</pre>
