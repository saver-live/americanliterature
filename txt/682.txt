<p>Athwart the bed<br> I watch the moonbeams cast a trail<br> So bright, so cold, so frail,<br> That for a space it gleams<br> Like hoar-frost on the margin of my dreams.<br> I raise my head, --<br> The splendid moon I see:<br> Then droop my head,<br> And sink to dreams of thee --<br> My Fatherland, of thee! <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
