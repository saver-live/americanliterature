<p><b>The Story of A Fierce Bad Rabbit</b></p><p>This is a fierce bad Rabbit; look at his savage whiskers and his claws and his turned-up tail.</p><p>This is a nice gentle Rabbit. His mother has given him a carrot.</p><p>The bad Rabbit would like some carrot.</p><p>He doesn't say "Please." He takes it!</p><p>And he scratches the good Rabbit very badly.</p><p>The good Rabbit creeps away and hides in a hole. It feels sad.</p><p>This is a man with a gun.</p><p>He sees something sitting on a bench. He thinks it is a very funny bird!</p><p>He comes creeping up behind the trees.</p><p>And then he shoots--BANG!</p><p>This is what happens--</p><p>But this is all he finds on the bench when he rushes up with his gun.</p><p>The good Rabbit peeps out of its hole . . .</p><p>. . . and it sees the bad Rabbit tearing past--without any tail or whiskers!</p><p> </p><p><br> </p><p> </p>
