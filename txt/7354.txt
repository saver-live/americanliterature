<p> The gothic novel <em><b>Frankenstein; or, The Modern Prometheus</b></em> was first published anonymously in 1818 in London. Mary Shelley started to write the novel when she was just 19, published when she was 21. </p><p> The story is so well renowned that there is no point in summarizing it here; I think it is difficult for any child to reach six or seven years old without gaining the knowledge that the story is about a scientific experiment gone wrong when Dr. Frankenstien creates a man (cue the visual of a lightning striking animating the monster). Like <a href="/author/bram-stoker/bio-books-stories">Bram Stoker's</a> <a href="/author/bram-stoker/book/dracula/summary">Dracula</a>, the story has become part of modern culture.
