<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Think no more, lad; laugh, be jolly:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Why should men make haste to die?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Empty heads and tongues a-talking
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Make the rough road easy walking,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the feather pate of folly
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Bears the falling sky.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Oh, 'tis jesting, dancing, drinking
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spins the heavy world around.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If young hearts were not so clever,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Oh, they would be young for ever:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Think no more; 'tis only thinking
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lays lads underground.
</pre>
