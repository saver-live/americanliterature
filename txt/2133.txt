<p>SO, so, break off this last lamenting kiss,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Which sucks two souls, and vapours both away;<br> Turn, thou ghost, that way, and let me turn this,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And let ourselves benight our happiest day.<br> We ask none leave to love; nor will we owe<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Any so cheap a death as saying, "Go."<br> <br> Go; and if that word have not quite killed thee,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Ease me with death, by bidding me go too.<br> Or, if it have, let my word work on me,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And a just office on a murderer do.<br> Except it be too late, to kill me so,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Being double dead, going, and bidding, "Go."</p>
