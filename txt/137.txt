<p>A Mule had had a long rest and much good feeding. He was feeling very vigorous indeed, and pranced around loftily, holding his head high.</p><p>"My father certainly was a full-blooded racer," he said. "I can feel that distinctly."</p><p>Next day he was put into harness again and that evening he was very downhearted indeed.</p><p>"I was mistaken," he said. "My father was an Ass after all."</p><p class="center"><i>Be sure of your pedigree before you boast of it.</i></p><p> </p><p><br> </p><p> </p>
