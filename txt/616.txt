<p>On Mirror Lake outspread for miles and miles, <br> The lotus lilies in full blossom teem. <br> In fifth moon Xi Shi gathers them with smiles, <br> Watchers o'erwhelm the bank of Yuoye Stream. <br> Her boat turns back without waiting moonrise <br> To yoyal house amid amorous sighs. <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
