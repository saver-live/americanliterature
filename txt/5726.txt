<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Solemnly, mournfully,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Dealing its dole,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Curfew Bell
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Is beginning to toll.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Cover the embers,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And put out the light;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Toil comes with the morning,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And rest with the night.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Dark grow the windows,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And quenched is the fire;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sound fades into silence,--
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All footsteps retire.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No voice in the chambers,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No sound in the hall!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sleep and oblivion
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Reign over all!


&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;II.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The book is completed,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And closed, like the day;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the hand that has written it
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lays it away.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Dim grow its fancies;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Forgotten they lie;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Like coals in the ashes,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They darken and die.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Song sinks into silence,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The story is told,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The windows are darkened,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The hearth-stone is cold.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Darker and darker
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The black shadows fall;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sleep and oblivion
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Reign over all.
</pre>
