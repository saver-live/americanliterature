<p>Where crowns a purple haze<br> Ashimmer in sunlight rays<br> The hill called Incense-Burner Peak, from far<br> To see, hung o'er the torrent's wall,<br> That waterfall<br> Vault sheer three thousand feet, you'd say<br> The Milky Way<br> Was tumbling from the high heavens, star on star <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
