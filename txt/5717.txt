<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How cold are thy baths, Apollo!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Cried the African monarch, the splendid,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As down to his death in the hollow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Dark dungeons of Rome he descended,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Uncrowned, unthroned, unattended;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How cold are thy baths, Apollo!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How cold are thy baths, Apollo!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Cried the Poet, unknown, unbefriended,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As the vision, that lured him to follow,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With the mist and the darkness blended,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the dream of his life was ended;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How cold are thy baths, Apollo!
</pre>
