&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;BIRDS OF PASSAGE

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Black shadows fall
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the lindens tall,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That lift aloft their massive wall
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Against the southern sky;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And from the realms
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the shadowy elms
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A tide-like darkness overwhelms
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The fields that round us lie.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But the night is fair,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And everywhere
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A warm, soft vapor fills the air,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And distant sounds seem near,

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And above, in the light
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the star-lit night,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Swift birds of passage wing their flight
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through the dewy atmosphere.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I hear the beat
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of their pinions fleet,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As from the land of snow and sleet
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They seek a southern lea.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I hear the cry
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of their voices high
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Falling dreamily through the sky,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But their forms I cannot see.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O, say not so!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Those sounds that flow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In murmurs of delight and woe
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Come not from wings of birds.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They are the throngs
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the poet's songs,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murmurs of pleasures, and pains, and wrongs,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The sound of winged words.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This is the cry
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of souls, that high
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On toiling, beating pinions, fly,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Seeking a warmer clime,

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From their distant flight
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through realms of light
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It falls into our world of night,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With the murmuring sound of rhyme.
