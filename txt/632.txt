<p>As the two of us drink<br> together, while mountain<br> flowers blossom beside, we<br> down one cup after the other<br> until I am drunk and sleepy<br> so that you better go!<br> Tomorrow if you feel like it<br> do come and bring your lute<br> along with you! <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
