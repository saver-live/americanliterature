<p>Sophacles's Theban plays include <span class="al-title">Oedipus the King</span>, <span class="al-title">Oedipus at Colonus</span>, and <span class="al-title">Antigone</span>. He wrote <span class="al-title">Oedipus at Colonus</span> last (406 BCE), shortly before his death, though the play is second in chronological order of events describing the end of Oedipus' tragic life.
