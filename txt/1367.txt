<p><cite class="al-title">Alice in Wonderland</cite> is the abbreviated titled of Lewis Carroll's beloved and fantastical adventure novel, <cite class="al-title">The Adventures of Alice in Wonderland</cite>, published in 1865. Follow Alice down the rabbit-hole to a world that gets "curiouser and curiouser!" The White Rabbit, Mad Hatter, March Hare, Dormouse, Cheshire Cat, and the Queen of Hearts are among the memorable characters encountered by Alice. This strange and wondrous tale is filled with wit and humor, often read by students in grades 4 - 5. Enjoy Carroll's much shorter version for younger children: <a href="/author/lewis-carroll/book/alices-adventures-under-ground/summary">Alice's Adventures Under Ground</a>, which features handwritten text and unique sketches by Carroll himself.</p><p>Carroll's original book is featured in our collection of <a href="/books-for-young-readers">Books for Young Readers</a></p><p><img alt="Alice in Wonderland caterpillar" title="Alice in Wonderland caterpillar" itemprop="image" src="https://assets.americanliterature.com/al/images/book/alices-adventures-in-wonderland-caterpillar.jpg" style="max-width:400px; float:right; margin-right: 12px; margin-bottom: 12px;" data-pagespeed-url-hash="2531797909" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"> <img alt="Alice's Adventures in Wonderland cover" title="Alice's Adventures in Wonderland cover" itemprop="image" src="https://assets.americanliterature.com/kids-stories/alices-adventures-in-wonderland.jpg" style="max-width:400px; float:right; margin-right: 12px; margin-bottom: 12px;" data-pagespeed-url-hash="554302296" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"> </p><p><a href="chapter-1">Chapter 1</a></p><p><a href="chapter-2">Chapter 2</a></p><p><a href="chapter-3">Chapter 3</a></p><p><a href="chapter-4">Chapter 4</a></p><p><a href="chapter-5">Chapter 5</a></p><p><a href="chapter-6">Chapter 6</a></p><p><a href="chapter-7">Chapter 7</a></p><p><a href="chapter-8">Chapter 8</a></p><p><a href="chapter-9">Chapter 9</a></p><p><a href="chapter-10">Chapter 10</a></p><p><a href="chapter-11">Chapter 11</a></p><p><a href="chapter-12">Chapter 12</a></p><p style="margin-top: 26px;"> Return to the <a href="/author/lewis-carroll">Lewis Carroll</a> library. </p>
