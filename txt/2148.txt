<p><span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>BEFORE I sigh my last gasp, let me breathe,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Great Love, some legacies; I here bequeath<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Mine eyes to Argus, if mine eyes can see;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>If they be blind, then, Love, I give them thee;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>My tongue to Fame; to ambassadors mine ears;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:3em;">&nbsp;</span>To women, or the sea, my tears;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>Thou, Love, hast taught me heretofore<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>By making me serve her who had twenty more,<br> That I should give to none, but such as had too much before.<br> <br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>My constancy I to the planets give;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>My truth to them who at the court do live;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>My ingenuity and openness,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>To Jesuits; to buffoons my pensiveness;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>My silence to any, who abroad hath been;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Of Amsterdam; my best civility<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And courtship to an University;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>My modesty I give to soldiers bare;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:3em;">&nbsp;</span>My patience let gamesters share:<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>Thou, Love, taught'st me, by making me<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Which were my friends; mine industry to foes;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>To schoolmen I bequeath my doubtfulness;<br> <span style="display:inline-block;
