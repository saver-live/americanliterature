<p><cite class="al-title">Carmilla</cite> (1872) is Le Fanu's gripping vampire tale with a twist, the vampire is a young lesbian girl! Le Fanu's novel heavily influenced <a href="/author/bram-stoker/bio-books-stories" title="Gothic Author:Bram Stoker">Bram Stoker's</a> <a href="/author/bram-stoker/book/dracula/summary" title="Gothic Novel: Dracula">Dracula</a> and inspired several films. It reminds us of the 2008 Swedish horror drama with the young girl vampire, <a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1139797/">Let the Right One In</a>.
