<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Viswamitra the Magician,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By his spells and incantations,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Up to Indra's realms elysian
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Raised Trisanku, king of nations.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Indra and the gods offended
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hurled him downward, and descending
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the air he hung suspended,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With these equal powers contending.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thus by aspirations lifted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By misgivings downward driven,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Human hearts are tossed and drifted
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Midway between earth and heaven.
</pre>
