<p><cite class="al-title">Abigail Adams and Her Times</cite> (1917) is a chapter book biography intended for young people, written by Laura E. Richards. Richards wrote several other biographies for young people, as well as nonsense poems and short stories for readers of all ages. Children will enjoy learning that during Abigail's time, they made pumpkins into "pyes" and milk was one penny a quart! <img alt="Abigail Adams and Her Times, husband John Adams" title="Abigail Adams and Her Times, husband John Adams" itemprop="image" src="https://assets.americanliterature.com/al/images/book/john-adams.jpg" style="max-width:250px;
