<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit here that reignest!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit here that painest!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit here that burneth!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit here that mourneth!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit! I bow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;My forehead low,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Enshaded with thy pinions!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit! I look
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All passion struck,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Into thy pale dominions!


&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit here that laughest!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit here that quaffest!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit here that danceth!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit here that pranceth!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit! with thee
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I join in the glee,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;While nudging the elbow of Momus!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spirit! I flush
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With a Bacchanal blush,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Just fresh from the banquet of Comus!
</pre>
