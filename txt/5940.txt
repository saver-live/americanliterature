&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The sea awoke at midnight from its sleep,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And round the pebbly beaches far and wide
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I heard the first wave of the rising tide
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Rush onward with uninterrupted sweep;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A voice out of the silence of the deep,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A sound mysteriously multiplied
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As of a cataract from the mountain's side,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Or roar of winds upon a wooded steep.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So comes to us at times, from the unknown
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And inaccessible solitudes of being,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The rushing of the sea-tides of the soul;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And inspirations, that we deem our own,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Are some divine foreshadowing and foreseeing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of things beyond our reason or control.
