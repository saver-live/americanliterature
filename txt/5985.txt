<pre>[Greek quotation]

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I heard the trailing garments of the Night
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Sweep through her marble halls!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I saw her sable skirts all fringed with light
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; From the celestial walls!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I felt her presence, by its spell of might,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Stoop o'er me from above;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The calm, majestic presence of the Night,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; As of the one I love.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I heard the sounds of sorrow and delight,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The manifold, soft chimes,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That fill the haunted chambers of the Night
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Like some old poet's rhymes.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the cool cisterns of the midnight air
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; My spirit drank repose;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The fountain of perpetual peace flows there,--
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; From those deep cisterns flows.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O holy Night! from thee I learn to bear
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; What man has borne before!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thou layest thy finger on the lips of Care,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; And they complain no more.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Peace!&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Peace!&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Orestes-like I breathe this prayer!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Descend with broad-winged flight,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The welcome, the thrice-prayed for, the most fair,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The best-beloved Night!
