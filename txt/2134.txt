<p>Mark but this flea, and mark in this,<br> How little that which thou deniest me is;<br> It suck'd me first, and now sucks thee,<br> And in this flea our two bloods mingled be.<br> Thou know'st that this cannot be said<br> A sin, nor shame, nor loss of maidenhead;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Yet this enjoys before it woo,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And pamper'd swells with one blood made of two;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And this, alas! is more than we would do.<br> <br> O stay, three lives in one flea spare,<br> Where we almost, yea, more than married are.<br> This flea is you and I, and this<br> Our marriage bed, and marriage temple is.<br> Though parents grudge, and you, we're met,<br> And cloister'd in these living walls of jet.<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Though use make you apt to kill me,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Let not to that self-murder added be,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And sacrilege, three sins in killing three.<br> <br> Cruel and sudden, hast thou since<br> Purpled thy nail in blood of innocence?<br> Wherein could this flea guilty be,<br> Except in that drop which it suck'd from thee?<br> Yet thou triumph'st, and say'st that thou<br> Find'st not thyself nor me the weaker now.<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>'Tis true; then learn how false fears be;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Just so much honour, when thou yield'st to me,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Will waste, as this flea's death took life from thee.</p>
