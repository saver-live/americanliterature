Blow, March, blow!
Go, Winter, go!
Drive away,
Strive away,
Blow, March, blow!

Blow, March, blow!
Grow, grass, grow!
Crocus-cup,
Twinkle up;
Blow, March, blow!

Blow, March, blow!
Flow, water, flow!
River, run,
Just for fun,
Blow, March, blow!
