<p><cite class="al-title">The Three Musketeers</cite> was published in 1844, about the adventures of d'Artagnan, who travels to Paris to join the Musketeers of the Guard, named Athos, Porthos, and Aramis. Dumas' historic adventure novel celebrates fighting injustices and absurdities of the Old Regime, at a time when animosity between French republicans and monarchists was still fierce.</p><p><a href="1-the-three-presents-of-dartagnan-the-elder">1.
