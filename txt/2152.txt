<p>NOW thou hast loved me one whole day,<br> To-morrow when thou leavest, what wilt thou say?<br> Wilt thou then antedate some new-made vow?<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Or say that now<br> We are not just those persons which we were?<br> Or that oaths made in reverential fear<br> Of Love, and his wrath, any may forswear?<br> Or, as true deaths true marriages untie,<br> So lovers' contracts, images of those,<br> Bind but till sleep, death's image, them unloose?<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Or, your own end to justify,<br> For having purposed change and falsehood, you<br> Can have no way but falsehood to be true?<br> Vain lunatic, against these 'scapes I could<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Dispute, and conquer, if I would;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Which I abstain to do,<br> For by to-morrow I may think so too.</p>
