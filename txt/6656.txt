<p><cite class="al-title">Beyond Good and Evil</cite> (1886) is one of Nietzsche's most studied and oft quoted works (see Chapter IV). We offer the translation by Helen Zimmern published in <cite class="al-title">The Complete Works of Friedrich Nietzsche (1909-1913)</cite>. He's a particular fan of numbering his thoughts and all-capital phrases which gets our attention, as if being shouted.
