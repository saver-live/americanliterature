<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Her strong enchantments failing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Her towers of fear in wreck,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Her limbecks dried of poisons
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the knife at her neck,

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Queen of air and darkness
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Begins to shrill and cry,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O young man, O my slayer,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To-morrow you shall die.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O Queen of air and darkness,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I think tis truth you say,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And I shall die to-morrow;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But you will die to-day.
</pre>
