<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All are architects of Fate,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Working in these walls of Time;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Some with massive deeds and great,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Some with ornaments of rhyme.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nothing useless is, or low;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Each thing in its place is best;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And what seems but idle show
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Strengthens and supports the rest.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For the structure that we raise,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Time is with materials filled;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Our to-days and yesterdays
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Are the blocks with which we build.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Truly shape and fashion these;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Leave no yawning gaps between;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Think not, because no man sees,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Such things will remain unseen.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the elder days of Art,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Builders wrought with greatest care
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Each minute and unseen part;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For the Gods see everywhere.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Let us do our work as well,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Both the unseen and the seen;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Make the house, where Gods may dwell,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Beautiful, entire, and clean.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Else our lives are incomplete,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Standing in these walls of Time,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Broken stairways, where the feet
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Stumble as they seek to climb.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Build to-day, then, strong and sure,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With a firm and ample base;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And ascending and secure
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Shall to-morrow find its place.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thus alone can we attain
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To those turrets, where the eye
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sees the world as one vast plain,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And one boundless reach of sky.
</pre>
