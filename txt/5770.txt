<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nowhere such a devious stream,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Save in fancy or in dream,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Winding slow through bush and brake
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Links together lake and lake.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Walled with woods or sandy shelf,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ever doubling on itself
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Flows the stream, so still and slow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That it hardly seems to flow.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Never errant knight of old,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lost in woodland or on wold,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Such a winding path pursued
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through the sylvan solitude.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Never school-boy in his quest
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;After hazel-nut or nest,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through the forest in and out
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Wandered loitering thus about.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the mirror of its tide
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tangled thickets on each side
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hang inverted, and between
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Floating cloud or sky serene.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Swift or swallow on the wing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Seems the only living thing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Or the loon, that laughs and flies
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Down to those reflected skies.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Silent stream! thy Indian name
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Unfamiliar is to fame;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For thou hidest here alone,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Well content to be unknown.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But thy tranquil waters teach
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Wisdom deep as human speech,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Moving without haste or noise
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In unbroken equipoise.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Though thou turnest no busy mill,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And art ever calm and still,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Even thy silence seems to say
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To the traveller on his way:--

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Traveller, hurrying from the heat
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the city, stay thy feet!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Rest awhile, nor longer waste
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Life with inconsiderate haste!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Be not like a stream that brawls
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Loud with shallow waterfalls,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But in quiet self-control
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Link together soul and soul"
</pre>
