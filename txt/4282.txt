<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where be ye going, you Devon maid?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And what have ye there i' the basket?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ye tight little fairy, just fresh from the dairy,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Will ye give me some cream if I ask it?


&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I love your meads, and I love your flowers,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And I love your junkets mainly,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But 'hind the door, I love kissing more,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O look not so disdainly!


&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I love your hills, and I love your dales,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And I love your flocks a-bleating;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But O, on the heather to lie together,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With both our hearts a-beating!


&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I'll put your basket all safe in a nook,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Your shawl I'll hang up on this willow,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And we will sigh in the daisy's eye,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And kiss on a grass-green pillow.
</pre>
