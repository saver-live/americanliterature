<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Say, lad, have you things to do?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quick then, while your day's at prime.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quick, and if 'tis work for two,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Here am I, man: now's your time.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Send me now, and I shall go;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Call me, I shall hear you call;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Use me ere they lay me low
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where a man's no use at all;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ere the wholesome flesh decay,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the willing nerve be numb,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the lips lack breath to say,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"No, my lad, I cannot come."
</pre>
