<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Under the walls of Monterey
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;At daybreak the bugles began to play,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the mist of the morning damp and gray,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;These were the words they seemed to say:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Come forth to thy death,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith!"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Forth he came, with a martial tread;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Firm was his step, erect his head;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He who so well the bugle played,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Could not mistake the words it said:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Come forth to thy death,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith!"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He looked at the earth, he looked at the sky,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He looked at the files of musketry,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And he said, with a steady voice and eye,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Take good aim; I am ready to die!"
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thus challenges death
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Twelve fiery tongues flashed straight and red,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Six leaden balls on their errand sped;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Falls to the ground, but he is not dead;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;His name was not stamped on those balls of lead,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And they only scath
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Three balls are in his breast and brain,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But he rises out of the dust again,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The water he drinks has a bloody stain;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"O kill me, and put me out of my pain!"
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In his agony prayeth
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Forth dart once more those tongues of flame,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the bugler has died a death of shame,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;His soul has gone back to whence it came,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And no one answers to the name,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When the Sergeant saith,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Victor Galbraith!"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Under the walls of Monterey
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By night a bugle is heard to play,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Victor Galbraith!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through the mist of the valley damp and gray
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The sentinels hear the sound, and say,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"That is the wraith
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of Victor Galbraith!"
