<pre>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The sigh that heaves the grasses
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Whence thou wilt never rise
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Is of the air that passes
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And knows not if it sighs.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The diamond tears adorning
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thy low mound on the lea,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Those are the tears of morning,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That weeps, but not for thee.
</pre>
