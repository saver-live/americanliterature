<p><a href="/author/william-shakespeare/bio-books-stories">William Shakespeare</a> wrote the comedy <cite class="al-title">Love's Labour's Lost</cite> in the mid-1590s. It was first published in 1598. </p><p>The noblemen, Berowne, Dumaine, and Longaville, vow to devote themselves to three years of study, promising not to give in to the company of women.
