<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When I watch the living meet,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the moving pageant file
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Warm and breathing through the street
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where I lodge a little while,

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If the heats of hate and lust
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the house of flesh are strong,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Let me mind the house of dust
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where my sojourn shall be long.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the nation that is not
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nothing stands that stood before;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There revenges are forgot,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the hater hates no more;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lovers lying two and two
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ask not whom they sleep beside,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the bridegroom all night through
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Never turns him to the bride.
</pre>
