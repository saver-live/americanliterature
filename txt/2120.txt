<p>SWEETEST love, I do not go,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>For weariness of thee,<br> Nor in hope the world can show<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>A fitter love for me;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>But since that I<br> At the last must part, 'tis best,<br> Thus to use myself in jest<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>By feigned deaths to die.<br> <br> Yesternight the sun went hence,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And yet is here to-day;<br> He hath no desire nor sense,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Nor half so short a way;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>Then fear not me,<br> But believe that I shall make<br> Speedier journeys, since I take<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>More wings and spurs than he.<br> <br> O how feeble is man's power,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>That if good fortune fall,<br> Cannot add another hour,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Nor a lost hour recall;<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>But come bad chance,<br> And we join to it our strength,<br> And we teach it art and length,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Itself o'er us to advance.<br> <br> When thou sigh'st, thou sigh'st not wind,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>But sigh'st my soul away;<br> When thou weep'st, unkindly kind,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>My life's blood doth decay.<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>It cannot be<br> That thou lovest me as thou say'st,<br> If in thine my life thou waste,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>That art the best of me.<br> <br> Let not thy divining heart<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Forethink me any ill;<br> Destiny may take thy part,<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>And may thy fears fulfil.<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:2em;">&nbsp;</span>But think that we<br> Are but turn'd aside to sleep.<br> They who one another keep<br> <span style="display:inline-block; width:1em;">&nbsp;</span>Alive, ne'er parted be.</p>
