<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Non nobis Domine!,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Not unto us, O Lord!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Praise or Glory be
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of any deed or word;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For in Thy Judgment lies
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To crown or bring to nought
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All knowledge or device
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That Man has reached or wrought.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And we confess our blame,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How all too high we hold
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That noise which men call Fame,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That dross which men call Gold.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For these we undergo
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Our hot and godless days,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But in our hearts we know
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Not unto us the Praise.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O Power by Whom we live,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Creator, Judge, and Friend,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Upholdingly forgive
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nor fail us at the end:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But grant us well to see
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In all our piteous ways,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Non nobis Domine!,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Not unto us the Praise!
</pre>
