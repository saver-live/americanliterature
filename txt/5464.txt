<p><cite class="al-title">Spirits in Bondage: A Cycle of Lyrics</cite> (1919) was <cite class="al-author">C.S. Lewis</cite>' first published book, a rather dark collection of poems using the pseudonym, <cite class="al-author">Clive Hamilton</cite>. Its attitudes and tone vary considerably from his many Christian books he would later publish.
