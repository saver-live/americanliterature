<p>Peach-tree flowers over rising waters.<br> White drowned stones, then free again.<br> Wistaria-blossom on quivering branches.<br> Clear blue sky. The waxing moon.<br> How many tight-coiled scrolls of bracken,<br> On green tracks where I once walked?<br> When I'm back from exile in Yeh-lang,<br> There I'll transmute my bones to gold. <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
