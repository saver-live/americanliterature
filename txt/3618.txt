<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;White in the moon the long road lies,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The moon stands blank above;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;White in the moon the long road lies
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That leads me from my love.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Still hangs the hedge without a gust,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Still, still the shadows stay:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;My feet upon the moonlit dust
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Pursue the ceaseless way.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The world is round, so travellers tell,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And straight though reach the track,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Trudge on, trudge on, 'twill all be well,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The way will guide one back.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But ere the circle homeward hies
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Far, far must it remove:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;White in the moon the long road lies
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That leads me from my love.
</pre>
