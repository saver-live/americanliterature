<p>A different setting for his familiar theme of "survival in the wilderness, Jack London's 1915 novel was called <span class="al-title">The Star Rover</span> in the U.S., but in the United Kingdom, the novel was titled <span class="al-title">The Jacket</span>. It is a brutal story about a professor named Darrell Standing, who is serving a life sentence in San Quentin for murder. Spoiler alert: he was framed.</p><p><span class="al-title">The Star Rover</span> was adapted into a movie directed by Edward Sloman in 1920, with the promotional poster promising to "impart a triple punch" with "mystery, romance, and vibrant drama."</p><p><img alt="The Star Rover Movie Poster 1920" title="The Star Rover movie poster 1920" itemprop="image" style="float:left; margin-right: 12px; margin-bottom: 10px;" src="https://assets.americanliterature.com/al/images/book/the-star-rover-b.jpg" data-pagespeed-url-hash="3409171543" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"> </p><p><a href="chapter-i">Chapter I</a></p><p><a href="chapter-ii">Chapter II</a></p><p><a href="chapter-iii">Chapter III</a></p><p><a href="chapter-iv">Chapter IV</a></p><p><a href="chapter-v">Chapter V</a></p><p><a href="chapter-vi">Chapter VI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-vii">Chapter VII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-viii">Chapter VIII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-ix">Chapter IX</a></p><p><a href="chapter-x">Chapter X</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xi">Chapter XI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xii">Chapter XII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xiii">Chapter XIII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xiv">Chapter XIV</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xv">Chapter XV</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xvi">Chapter XVI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xvii">Chapter XVII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xviii">Chapter XVIII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xix">Chapter XIX</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xx">Chapter XX</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxi">Chapter XXI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxii">Chapter XXII</a></p><p><a href="footnote">Footnote</a></p><p style="margin-top: 26px;"> Return to the <a href="/author/jack-london">Jack London</a> library. </p>
