<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Souls of Poets dead and gone,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What Elysium have ye known,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Happy field or mossy cavern,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Choicer than the Mermaid Tavern?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Have ye tippled drink more fine
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Than mine hosts Canary wine?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Or are fruits of Paradise
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sweeter than those dainty pies
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of venison? O generous food!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Drest as though bold Robin Hood
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Would, with his maid Marian,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sup and bowse from horn and can.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I have heard that on a day
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Mine hosts sign-board flew away,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nobody knew whither, till
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;An astrologers old quill
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To a sheepskin gave the story,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Said he saw you in your glory,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Underneath a new old sign
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sipping beverage divine,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And pledging with contented smack
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Mermaid in the Zodiac.
