<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Into the darkness and the hush of night
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Slowly the landscape sinks, and fades away,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And with it fade the phantoms of the day,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The ghosts of men and things, that haunt the light,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The crowd, the clamor, the pursuit, the flight,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The unprofitable splendor and display,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The agitations, and the cares that prey
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Upon our hearts, all vanish out of sight.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The better life begins; the world no more
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Molests us; all its records we erase
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the dull common-place book of our lives,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That like a palimpsest is written o'er
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With trivial incidents of time and place,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And lo! the ideal, hidden beneath, revives.
</pre>
