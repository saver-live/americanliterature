<p><cite class="al-title">When William Came: A Story of London Under the Hohenzollerns</cite> (1913) is SAKI's <a href="/dystopian-stories">Dystopian novel</a> predicting a futuristic "Great War" between Germany and Great Britain in which Germany is victorious and occupies London. "William" refers to Kaiser Wilhelm II.
