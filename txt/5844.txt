<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The brooklet came from the mountain,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As sang the bard of old,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Running with feet of silver
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Over the sands of gold!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Far away in the briny ocean
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There rolled a turbulent wave,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Now singing along the sea-beach,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Now howling along the cave.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the brooklet has found the billow
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Though they flowed so far apart,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And has filled with its freshness and sweetness
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That turbulent bitter heart!
</pre>
