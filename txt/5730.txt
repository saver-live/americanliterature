<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sleep, comrades, sleep and rest
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On this Field of the Grounded Arms,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where foes no more molest,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nor sentry's shot alarms!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ye have slept on the ground before,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And started to your feet
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;At the cannon's sudden roar,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Or the drum's redoubling beat.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But in this camp of Death
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No sound your slumber breaks;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Here is no fevered breath,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No wound that bleeds and aches.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All is repose and peace,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Untrampled lies the sod;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The shouts of battle cease,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It is the Truce of God!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Rest, comrades, rest and sleep!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The thoughts of men shall be
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As sentinels to keep
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Your rest from danger free.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Your silent tents of green
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;We deck with fragrant flowers;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yours has the suffering been,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The memory shall be ours.
</pre>
