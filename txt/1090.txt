Mr. Bjornson has designated <span class="al-person">Prof. Rasmus B. Anderson</span> as his American translator, cooperates with him, and revises each work before it is translated, thus giving his personal attention to this edition.</p><p><a href="chapter-i">CHAPTER I</a></p><p><a href="chapter-ii">CHAPTER II</a></p><p><a href="chapter-iii">CHAPTER III</a></p><p><a href="chapter-iv">CHAPTER IV</a></p><p><a href="chapter-v">CHAPTER V</a></p><p><a href="chapter-vi">CHAPTER VI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-vii">CHAPTER VII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-viii">CHAPTER VIII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-ix">CHAPTER IX</a></p><p><a href="chapter-x">CHAPTER X</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xi">CHAPTER XI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xii">CHAPTER XII</a></p><p style="margin-top: 26px;"> Return to the <a href="/author/bjrnstjerne-bjrnson">Bjørnstjerne Bjørnson</a> library. </p>
