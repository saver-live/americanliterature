<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From far, from eve and morning
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And yon twelve-winded sky,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The stuff of life to knit me
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blew hither: here am I.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Now- for a breath I tarry
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nor yet disperse apart-
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Take my hand quick and tell me,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What have you in your heart.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Speak now, and I will answer;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How shall I help you, say;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ere to the wind's twelve quarters
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I take my endless way.
</pre>
