<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ah! what pleasant visions haunt me
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As I gaze upon the sea!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All the old romantic legends,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All my dreams, come back to me.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sails of silk and ropes of sandal,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Such as gleam in ancient lore;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the singing of the sailors,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the answer from the shore!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Most of all, the Spanish ballad
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Haunts me oft, and tarries long,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the noble Count Arnaldos
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the sailor's mystic song.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Like the long waves on a sea-beach,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where the sand as silver shines,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With a soft, monotonous cadence,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Flow its unrhymed lyric lines:--

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Telling how the Count Arnaldos,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With his hawk upon his hand,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Saw a fair and stately galley,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Steering onward to the land;--

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How he heard the ancient helmsman
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Chant a song so wild and clear,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That the sailing sea-bird slowly
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Poised upon the mast to hear,

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Till his soul was full of longing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And he cried, with impulse strong,--
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Helmsman! for the love of heaven,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Teach me, too, that wondrous song!"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Wouldst thou,"--so the helmsman answered,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Learn the secret of the sea?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Only those who brave its dangers
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Comprehend its mystery!"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In each sail that skims the horizon,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In each landward-blowing breeze,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I behold that stately galley,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hear those mournful melodies;

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Till my soul is full of longing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For the secret of the sea,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the heart of the great ocean
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sends a thrilling pulse through me.
</pre>
