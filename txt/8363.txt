<p><cite class="al-title">The History of Henry Esmond, Esq.: A Colonel in the Service of Her Majesty, Queen Anne</cite> is an historical novel by Thackeray, in which Henry Esmond tells his own story as a memoir. While the author credits state, "Written By Himself" it is clearly Thackeray's authorship, and a fine example of a Victorian historical novel with interwoven events and characters, both real and imagined, from 17th- and 18th-century England. <a href="/author/oscar-wilde">Oscar Wilde</a> observed: <i>"Thackeray's <cite class="al-title">Esmond</cite> is a beautiful work of art because he wrote it to please himself."</i></p><p><img alt="The History of Henry Esmond, Queen Anne" title="The History of Henry Esmond, Queen Anne" itemprop="image" src="https://assets.americanliterature.com/al/images/book/the-history-of-henry-esmond-queen-anne.jpg" style="max-width:300px; float:right; margin-right: 12px; margin-bottom: 12px;" data-pagespeed-url-hash="2267596827" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"><img alt="The History of Henry Esmond, John Hoppner, Unknown British Officer, 1800" title="The History of Henry Esmond, John Hoppner, Unknown British Officer, 1800" itemprop="image" src="https://assets.americanliterature.com/al/images/book/the-history-of-henry-esmond-john-hoppner-unknown-british-officer.jpg" style="max-width:300px; float:right; margin-right: 12px; margin-bottom: 12px;" data-pagespeed-url-hash="1921883926" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"><img alt="The History of Henry Esmond, The Battle of Blenheim, 1704" title="The History of Henry Esmond, The Battle of Blenheim, 1704" itemprop="image" src="https://assets.americanliterature.com/al/images/book/the-history-of-henry-esmond-battle-of-blenheim-1704.jpg" style="max-width:400px; float:right; margin-right: 12px; margin-bottom: 12px;" data-pagespeed-url-hash="2283585976" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"> </p><p><a href="dedication">Dedication</a></p><p><a href="preface-the-esmonds-of-virginia">Preface -
