<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The half-moon westers low, my love,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the wind brings up the rain;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And wide apart lie we, my love,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And seas between the twain.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I know not if it rains, my love,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the land where you do lie;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And oh, so sound you sleep, my love,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You know no more than I.
</pre>
