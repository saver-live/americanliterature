<pre>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The sun is set; and in his latest beams
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yon little cloud of ashen gray and gold,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Slowly upon the amber air unrolled,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The falling mantle of the Prophet seems.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the dim headlands many a lighthouse gleams,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The street-lamps of the ocean; and behold,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O'erhead the banners of the night unfold;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The day hath passed into the land of dreams.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O summer day beside the joyous sea!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O summer day so wonderful and white,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So full of gladness and so full of pain!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Forever and forever shalt thou be
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To some the gravestone of a dead delight,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To some the landmark of a new domain.
</pre>
