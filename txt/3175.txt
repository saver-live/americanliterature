<p>Fraudulent dealings, accusations of witchcraft and sudden death. Nathaniel Hawthorne's novel, <cite class="al-title">House of Seven Gables</cite> serves them all up with style. Hawthorne explores guilt, retribution and atonement as the Pyncheon family faces life in their gloomy, seven gabled, New England mansion. Nathaniel Hawthorne's <cite class="al-title">The House of Seven Gables</cite> treads on familiar but masterful ground, examining the consequences of sin. </p><p>This book is featured in our collection of <a href="/twenty-five-great-american-novels">25 Great American Novels</a>. <i>The accompanying photo is The House of Seven Gables in 1915, Salem, Massachusetts.</i></p><p><a href="intro-and-preface">Intro and Preface</a></p><p><a href="the-old-pyncheon-family">The Old Pyncheon Family</a></p><p><a href="the-little-shop-window">The Little Shop-Window</a></p><p><a href="the-first-customer">The First Customer</a></p><p><a href="a-day-behind-the-counter">A Day Behind the Counter</a></p><p><a href="may-and-november">May and November</a></p><p><a href="maules-well">Maule's Well</a></p><p><a href="the-guest">The Guest</a></p><p><a href="the-pyncheon-of-to-day">The Pyncheon of To-day</a></p><p><a href="clifford-and-phoebe">Clifford and Phoebe</a></p><p><a href="the-pyncheon-garden">The Pyncheon Garden</a></p><p><a href="the-arched-window">The Arched Window</a></p><p><a href="the-daguerreotypist">The Daguerreotypist</a></p><p><a href="alice-pyncheon">Alice Pyncheon</a></p><p><a href="phoebes-good-by">Phoebe's Good-By</a></p><p><a href="the-scowl-and-smile">The Scowl and Smile</a></p><p><a href="cliffords-chamber">Clifford's Chamber</a></p><p><a href="the-flight-of-two-owls">The Flight of Two Owls</a></p><p><a href="governor-pyncheon">Governor Pyncheon</a></p><p><a href="alices-posies">Alice's Posies</a></p><p><a href="the-flower-of-eden">The Flower of Eden</a></p><p><a href="the-departure">The Departure</a></p><p style="margin-top: 26px;"> Return to the <a href="/author/nathaniel-hawthorne">Nathaniel Hawthorne</a> library. </p>
