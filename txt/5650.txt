<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It is autumn; not without,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But within me is the cold.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Youth and spring are all about;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It is I that have grown old.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Birds are darting through the air,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Singing, building without rest;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Life is stirring everywhere,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Save within my lonely breast.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There is silence: the dead leaves
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fall and rustle and are still;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Beats no flail upon the sheaves
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Comes no murmur from the mill.
</pre>
