<p><cite class="al-title">Anna Christie</cite> (1921) is a four-act play that earned O'Neill the 1922 Pulitzer Prize for Drama. We feature it in our collection of <a href="/pulitzer-prizes#pulitzer-drama">Pulitzer Prizes</a>. The protagonist is a former prostitute trying to turn her life around when she falls in love. Greta Garbo played Anna Christopherson in the 1930 film adaptation, pictured at right.
