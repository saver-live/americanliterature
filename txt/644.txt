<p>When we met the first time at Ch'ang-an<br> He called me the "Lost Immortal".<br> Then he loved the Way of Forgetting.<br> Now under the pine-trees he is dust.<br> His golden keepsake bought us wine.<br> Remembering, the tears run down my cheeks. <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
