<p> <b><em>Women in Love</em></b>, D.H Lawrence's sequel to <b>The Rainbow</b> (1915), continues to follow the lives of Ursula and Gudren Brangwen; two sisters living in the Midlands of England; a teacher and an artist respectively. They meet two local men, chool inspector Rupert Birkin and coal-mine heir Gerald Crich. Ursula and Birkin become romantically involved and Gudrun enters a love affair with Gerald. </p><p> Although tame by today's standards, <b><em>Women in Love</em></b> caused great deal of controversy over its sexual subject matter. </p><p><a href="sisters">Sisters</a></p><p><a href="shortlands">Shortlands</a></p><p><a href="class-room">Class-Room</a></p><p><a href="diver">Diver</a></p><p><a href="in-the-train">In The Train</a></p><p><a href="creme-de-menthe">Creme De Menthe</a></p><p><a href="fetish">Fetish</a></p><p><a href="breadalby">Breadalby</a></p><p><a href="coal-dust">Coal-Dust</a></p><p><a href="sketch-book">Sketch-Book</a></p><p><a href="an-island">An Island</a></p><p><a href="carpeting">Carpeting</a></p><p><a href="mino">Mino</a></p><p><a href="water-party">Water-Party</a></p><p><a href="sunday-evening">Sunday Evening</a></p><p><a href="man-to-man">Man To Man</a></p><p><a href="the-industrial-magnate">The Industrial Magnate</a></p><p><a href="rabbit">Rabbit</a></p><p><a href="moony">Moony</a></p><p><a href="gladiatorial">Gladiatorial</a></p><p><a href="threshold">Threshold</a></p><p><a href="woman-to-woman">Woman To Woman</a></p><p><a href="excurse">Excurse</a></p><p><a href="death-and-love">Death And Love</a></p><p><a href="marriage-or-not">Marriage Or Not</a></p><p><a href="a-chair">A Chair</a></p><p><a href="flitting">Flitting</a></p><p><a href="gudrun-in-the-pompadour">Gudrun In The Pompadour</a></p><p><a href="continental">Continental</a></p><p><a href="snowed-up">Snowed Up</a></p><p><a href="exeunt">Exeunt</a></p><p style="margin-top: 26px;"> Return to the <a href="/author/d-h-lawrence">D.
