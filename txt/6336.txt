<p>Herman Melville wrote, <b><em>Omoo: A Narrative of Adventures in the South Seas</em></b>, in 1846 and it was published in 1847. The novel is autobiograhical in nature, based on his experiences sailing on the Australian whaler <em>Lucy Ann</em>, but it might be more accurately described as based upon personal experience and
