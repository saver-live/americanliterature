<p>"Short live the King!" Shakespeare's tragedy is about the short life and rule of Henry V, who served as King from 1413 to 1422 and died at 36. <span class="al-title">King Henry V</span>, also called <span class="al-title">Henry V</span> was probably written by Shakespeare in 1599.
