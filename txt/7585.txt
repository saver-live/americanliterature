<p> Why are those tears in your eyes, my child?<br> How horrid of them to be always scolding you for nothing?<br> You have stained your fingers and face with ink while writing--is that why they call you dirty?<br> O, fie! Would they dare to call the full moon dirty because it has smudged its face with ink?<br> For every little trifle they blame you, my child. They are ready to find fault for nothing.<br> You tore your clothes while playing--is that why they call you untidy?<br> O, fie! What would they call an autumn morning that smiles through its ragged clouds?<br> Take no heed of what they say to you, my child.<br> Take no heed of what they say to you, my child.<br> They make a long list of your misdeeds. Everybody knows how you love sweet things--is that why they call you greedy?<br> O, fie! What then would they call us who love you?</p>
