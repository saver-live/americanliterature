<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When Mazarvan the Magician,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Journeyed westward through Cathay,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nothing heard he but the praises
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of Badoura on his way.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But the lessening rumor ended
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When he came to Khaledan,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There the folk were talking only
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of Prince Camaralzaman,

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So it happens with the poets:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Every province hath its own;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Camaralzaman is famous
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where Badoura is unknown.
</pre>
