<p>A Stag was chased by the hounds, and took refuge in a cave, where he hoped to be safe from his pursuers. Unfortunately the cave contained a Lion, to whom he fell an easy prey. "Unhappy that I am," he cried, "I am saved from the power of the dogs only to fall into the clutches of a Lion."</p><p> </p><p><i>Out of the frying-pan, into the fire.</i></p><p> </p><p><br> </p><p> </p>
