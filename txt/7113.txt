<p><cite class="al-title">The Circular Staircase</cite> (1908) by <cite class="al-author">Mary Roberts Rinehart</cite> is credited as the first "Had-I-But-Known" mystery book. It was adapted as a Broadway play, "The Bat" which premiered on August 23, 1920, and inspired Bob Kane's character "The Batman."
