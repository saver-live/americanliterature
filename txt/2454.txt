<p><small> The first five chapters of the Autobiography were composed in England in 1771, continued in 1784-5, and again in 1788, at which date he brought it down to 1757. After a most extraordinary series of adventures, the original form of the manuscript was finally printed by Mr. John Bigelow, and is here reproduced in recognition of its value as a picture of one of the most notable personalities of Colonial times, and of its acknowledged rank as one of the great autobiographies of the world.</small> </p><p><a href="1706-1757-i">I</a></p><p><a href="ii">II</a></p><p><a href="note-from-1788-iii">III</a></p><p><a href="last-year-1790-iv">IV</a></p><p><a href="chief-events-in-franklins-life-chief-events-in-franklins-life">Chief Events in Franklin's Life</a></p><p style="margin-top: 26px;"> Return to the <a href="/author/benjamin-franklin">Benjamin Franklin</a> library. </p>
