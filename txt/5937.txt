<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;White swan of cities, slumbering in thy nest
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So wonderfully built among the reeds
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the lagoon, that fences thee and feeds,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As sayeth thy old historian and thy guest!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;White water-lily, cradled and caressed
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By ocean streams, and from the silt and weeds
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lifting thy golden filaments and seeds,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thy sun-illumined spires, thy crown and crest!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;White phantom city, whose untrodden streets
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Are rivers, and whose pavements are the shifting
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Shadows of palaces and strips of sky;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I wait to see thee vanish like the fleets
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Seen in mirage, or towers of cloud uplifting
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In air their unsubstantial masonry.
</pre>
