<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Good Kosciusko, thy great name alone
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Is a full harvest whence to reap high feeling;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It comes upon us like the glorious pealing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of the wide spheres, an everlasting tone.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And now it tells me, that in worlds unknown, 
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The names of heroes, burst from clouds concealing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And changed to harmonies, for ever stealing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Through cloudless blue, and round each silver throne.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It tells me too, that on a happy day,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When some good spirit walks upon the earth, 
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thy name with Alfred's, and the great of yore
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Gently commingling, gives tremendous birth
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To a loud hymn, that sounds far, far away
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To where the great God lives for evermore.
</pre>
