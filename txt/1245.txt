<p>What's the best thing in the world?<br> June-rose, by May-dew impearled;<br> Sweet south-wind, that means no rain;<br> Truth, not cruel to a friend;<br> Pleasure, not in haste to end;<br> Beauty, not self-decked and curled<br> Till its pride is over-plain;<br> Love, when, so, you're loved again.<br> What's the best thing in the world?<br> --Something out of it, I think.</p>
