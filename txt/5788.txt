<pre>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I pace the sounding sea-beach and behold
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How the voluminous billows roll and run,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Upheaving and subsiding, while the sun
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Shines through their sheeted emerald far unrolled,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the ninth wave, slow gathering fold by fold
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All its loose-flowing garments into one,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Plunges upon the shore, and floods the dun
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Pale reach of sands, and changes them to gold.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So in majestic cadence rise and fall
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The mighty undulations of thy song,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O sightless bard, England's Maeonides!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And ever and anon, high over all
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Uplifted, a ninth wave superb and strong,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Floods all the soul with its melodious seas.
</pre>
