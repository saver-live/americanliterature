<p>Under the crescent moon's faint glow<br> The washerman's bat resounds afar,<br> And the autumn breeze sighs tenderly.<br> But my heart has gone to the Tartar war,<br> To bleak Kansuh and the steppes of snow,<br> Calling my husband back to me. <br></p>
<div class="poet" itemprop="author">
 Li Po
</div>
