<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Have I dreamed? or was it real,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What I saw as in a vision,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When to marches hymeneal
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the land of the Ideal
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Moved my thought o'er Fields Elysian?

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What! are these the guests whose glances
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Seemed like sunshine gleaming round me?
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;These the wild, bewildering fancies,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That with dithyrambic dances
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As with magic circles bound me?

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ah! how cold are their caresses!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Pallid cheeks, and haggard bosoms!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spectral gleam their snow-white dresses,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And from loose dishevelled tresses
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fall the hyacinthine blossoms!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O my songs! whose winsome measures
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Filled my heart with secret rapture!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Children of my golden leisures!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Must even your delights and pleasures
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fade and perish with the capture?

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fair they seemed, those songs sonorous,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When they came to me unbidden;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Voices single, and in chorus,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Like the wild birds singing o'er us
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the dark of branches hidden.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Disenchantment!&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Disillusion!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Must each noble aspiration
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Come at last to this conclusion,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Jarring discord, wild confusion,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lassitude, renunciation?

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Not with steeper fall nor faster,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the sun's serene dominions,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Not through brighter realms nor vaster,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In swift ruin and disaster,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Icarus fell with shattered pinions!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sweet Pandora! dear Pandora!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Why did mighty Jove create thee
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Coy as Thetis, fair as Flora,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Beautiful as young Aurora,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If to win thee is to hate thee?

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No, not hate thee! for this feeling
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of unrest and long resistance
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Is but passionate appealing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A prophetic whisper stealing
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O'er the chords of our existence.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Him whom thou dost once enamour,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thou, beloved, never leavest;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In life's discord, strife, and clamor,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Still he feels thy spell of glamour;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Him of Hope thou ne'er bereavest.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Weary hearts by thee are lifted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Struggling souls by thee are strengthened,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Clouds of fear asunder rifted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Truth from falsehood cleansed and sifted,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Lives, like days in summer, lengthened!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Therefore art thou ever clearer,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O my Sibyl, my deceiver!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For thou makest each mystery clearer,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And the unattained seems nearer,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When thou fillest my heart with fever!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Muse of all the Gifts and Graces!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Though the fields around us wither,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There are ampler realms and spaces,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where no foot has left its traces:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Let us turn and wander thither!
