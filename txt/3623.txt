<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With rue my heart is laden
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For golden friends I had,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For many a rose-lipt maiden
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And many a lightfoot lad.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By brooks too broad for leaping
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The lightfoot boys are laid;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The rose-lipt girls are sleeping
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In fields where roses fade.
</pre>
