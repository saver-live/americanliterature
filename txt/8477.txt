<p>The literal translation of this 1862 novel is <span class="al-title">Fathers and Children</span>. Turgenev's young protagonists are Arkady and Bazarov, both caustic young men who reject authority and social conventions-- much to Arkady's father Nikolai's horror-- which unleashes a whole series of scandals, and misunderstandings. Readers will come to appreciate the meaning of nihilism. The novel is often studied in high school grades 9-10. Its sequel, <a href="/author/ivan-s-turgenev/book/smoke/summary">Smoke</a> is Turgenev's 1867 novel, a critique of social reforms undertaken by the charlatan "intellectuals" who would shape Russia's future.</p><p><a href="biographical-note">Biographical Note</a></p><p><a href="criticisms-and-interpretations">Criticisms and Interpretations</a></p><p><a href="list-of-characters">List of Characters</a></p><p><a href="chapter-i">Chapter I</a></p><p><a href="chapter-ii">Chapter II</a></p><p><a href="chapter-iii">Chapter III</a></p><p><a href="chapter-iv">Chapter IV</a></p><p><a href="chapter-v">Chapter V</a></p><p><a href="chapter-vi">Chapter VI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-vii">Chapter VII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-viii">Chapter VIII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-ix">Chapter IX</a></p><p><a href="chapter-x">Chapter X</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xi">Chapter XI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xii">Chapter XII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xiii">Chapter XIII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xiv">Chapter XIV</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xv">Chapter XV</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xvi">Chapter XVI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xvii">Chapter XVII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xviii">Chapter XVIII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xix">Chapter XIX</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xx">Chapter XX</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxi">Chapter XXI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxii">Chapter XXII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxiii">Chapter XXIII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxiv">Chapter XXIV</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxv">Chapter XXV</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxvi">Chapter XXVI</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxvii">Chapter XXVII</a></p><p><a href="chapter-xxviii">Chapter XXVIII</a></p><p style="margin-top: 26px;"> Return to the <a href="/author/ivan-s-turgenev">Ivan S.
