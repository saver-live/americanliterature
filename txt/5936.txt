<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;DEDICATION

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;TO G.W.G.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With favoring winds, o'er sunlit seas,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;We sailed for the Hesperides,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The land where golden apples grow;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But that, ah! that was long ago.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How far, since then, the ocean streams
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Have swept us from that land of dreams,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That land of fiction and of truth,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The lost Atlantis of our youth!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Whither, oh, whither?&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Are not these
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The tempest-haunted Hebrides,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Where sea gulls scream, and breakers roar,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And wreck and sea-weed line the shore?

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ultima Thule!&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Utmost Isle!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Here in thy harbors for a while
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;We lower our sails; a while we rest
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From the unending, endless quest.
</pre>
