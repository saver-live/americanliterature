<p>Comprised of several intersecting stories and incorporating historical events like the beginning of railways, the 1832 Reform Act, and the death of King George IV, George Eliot created this fictitious town, the novel's namesake, <span class="al-title">Middlemarch, A Study of Provincial Life</span>. Eliot's novel was first published in eight installments in 1871-1872.
