<p><cite class="al-title">The Mystery of the Yellow Room</cite> was one of the first locked-room mysteries, published serially in France as <cite class="al-title">Le mystère de la chambre jaune</cite> in <cite class="al-periodical">L'Illustration</cite> from September 1907 to November 1907, and as a novel in 1908.
