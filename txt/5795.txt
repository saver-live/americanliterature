<pre>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Between the dark and the daylight,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When the night is beginning to lower,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Comes a pause in the day's occupations,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; That is known as the Children's Hour.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I hear in the chamber above me
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The patter of little feet,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The sound of a door that is opened,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And voices soft and sweet.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From my study I see in the lamplight,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Descending the broad hall stair,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Grave Alice, and laughing Allegra,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And Edith with golden hair.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A whisper, and then a silence:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yet I know by their merry eyes
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They are plotting and planning together
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To take me by surprise.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A sudden rush from the stairway,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A sudden raid from the hall!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;By three doors left unguarded
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They enter my castle wall!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They climb up into my turret
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O'er the arms and back of my chair;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If I try to escape, they surround me;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They seem to be everywhere.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They almost devour me with kisses,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Their arms about me entwine,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Till I think of the Bishop of Bingen
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In his Mouse-Tower on the Rhine!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Do you think, o blue-eyed banditti,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Because you have scaled the wall,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Such an old mustache as I am
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Is not a match for you all!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I have you fast in my fortress,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And will not let you depart,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But put you down into the dungeon
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the round-tower of my heart.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And there will I keep you forever,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yes, forever and a day,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Till the walls shall crumble to ruin,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And moulder in dust away!
</pre>
