<pre>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The holiest of all holidays are those
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Kept by ourselves in silence and apart;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The secret anniversaries of the heart,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When the full river of feeling overflows;--
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The happy days unclouded to their close;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The sudden joys that out of darkness start
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As flames from ashes; swift desires that dart
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Like swallows singing down each wind that blows!
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;White as the gleam of a receding sail,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;White as a cloud that floats and fades in air,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;White as the whitest lily on a stream,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;These tender memories are;--a Fairy Tale
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of some enchanted land we know not where,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But lovely as a landscape in a dream.
</pre>
